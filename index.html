Arowana Care Guides

Start with these core routines.

1. Feeding Strategy

  • Primary: pellets, prawns, earthworms, crickets
  • Occasional: chicken heart (not daily), superworms
  • 1–2 feedings / day, reduce during travel
  • Train young fish to accept floating food

2. Water Parameters

  • Temp: 28–30°C
  • pH: 6.5–7.2
  • Weekly 20–30% water change
  • Dechlorinate & match temp

3. Behaviour & Tank Mates

  • Monitor aggression when adding new Arowana
  • Compatible: Peacock Bass, Datnoid, Stingray (size match)
  • Avoid fin-nippers and small fish

Tank Setup & Equipment

Build once, then maintain easily.

Tank Size

Min 5 ft for single Arowana. For 3 Arowanas + Peacock Bass, go 7–8 ft, 60–70 cm height.

Filtration

Sump with 3–4 chambers, bio media, return pump. Add surface skimmer if using tanning light.

Lighting

Normal day light + tanning light cycles. Avoid 24/7 strong light → stress.

Backup / Vacation Mode

Auto feeder, stable temp, pre-measured food, caretaker notes.
